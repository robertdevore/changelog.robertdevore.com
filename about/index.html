About

Welcome to the CHANGELOG, the dedicated home for tracking updates across all of my projects.

I maintain and develop a range of plugins, tools, and software, and keeping up with release notes, feature additions, and fixes can be overwhelming--both for me and for users. Instead of cluttering my main blog with these updates, I created this space to serve as a streamlined, organized feed for all new releases.

Each post here reflects the latest changes to my plugins, from major feature rollouts to small quality-of-life improvements. While some entries may be a direct reflection of the changelogs themselves, I also plan to add context, insights, and additional resources over time.

This site is built with Stattic, my own static site generator, ensuring that updates are fast and lightweight - plus, who doesn't love dogfooding their own products?

If you use any of my plugins or follow my work, this is the best place to stay up to date on what's new.

Why CHANGELOG?

  • Keeps my main blog free of constant update posts.
  • Provides a dedicated place for plugin users to track releases.
  • Allows me to add more context, notes, and links beyond just a version update.

For plugin users, developers, and anyone interested in the evolution of my work, this site is the go-to resource. Stay tuned for more updates!
